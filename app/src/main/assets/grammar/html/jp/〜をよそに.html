〜をよそに

Explanation in Original Language

〜は自分とは無関係であるかのように、少しも気にしないで行動する。

Meaning and Nuances

This means acting “ignoring 〜” or “despite 〜,” treating it as irrelevant. It often implies indifference or detachment.

Usage in Context

Used in speech or writing to describe someone disregarding expectations or circumstances, sometimes critically.

Examples

親の反対をよそに、彼は結婚を決めた。

Ignoring his parents’ objections, he decided to marry.

Defiance.

雨をよそに、子供たちは外で遊び続けた。

Despite the rain, the kids kept playing outside.

Carefree behavior.

周囲の視線をよそに、彼女は大声で笑った。

Unconcerned by others’ stares, she laughed loudly.

Boldness.

Comparison to Similar Structures

〜にもかかわらず: “Despite,” more neutral than indifferent.

〜を無視して: “Ignoring,” more deliberate and active.

Cultural and Practical Notes

Can imply nonconformity, rare in collectivist Japan. Useful for N1 nuanced description.
