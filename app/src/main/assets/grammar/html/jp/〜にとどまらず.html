〜にとどまらず

Explanation in Original Language

〜の範囲に収まらないで、もっと広く及ぶ。

Meaning and Nuances

This indicates that something extends beyond 〜, surpassing an expected limit to a broader scope. It emphasizes expansiveness, often positively.

Usage in Context

Used in formal or semi-formal contexts like essays, reports, or speeches to show wide influence or impact.

Examples

彼の影響は家族にとどまらず、地域全体に広がった。

His influence extended beyond his family to the entire community.

Social impact.

技術革新は産業にとどまらず、生活様式を変えた。

Technological innovation went beyond industry, changing lifestyles.

Tech commentary.

議論は政治にとどまらず、倫理にも及んだ。

The debate extended beyond politics to ethics.

Intellectual discussion.

Comparison to Similar Structures

〜を超えて: Similar “beyond,” but less formal and broad.

〜だけでなく: Simpler “not only,” without the expansive nuance.

Cultural and Practical Notes

Reflects a tendency to highlight far-reaching effects in formal discourse. Key for N1 essays.
