〜いかんにかかわらず・いかんによらず・いかんをとわず

Explanation in Original Language

あることの成立に〜は関係ない・影響はない。

Meaning and Nuances

This means “regardless of 〜,” indicating that 〜 has no bearing on the outcome. All three forms are formal and interchangeable, with slight stylistic differences.

Usage in Context

Used in formal statements or rules to emphasize impartiality or universality.

Examples

年齢いかんにかかわらず、誰でも参加できる。

Regardless of age, anyone can participate.

Inclusive policy.

結果いかんによらず、努力は認められる。

Regardless of the outcome, effort will be recognized.

Encouragement.

天候いかんをとわず、工事は進められる。

The construction will proceed regardless of the weather.

Determination.

Comparison to Similar Structures

〜に関係なく: Similar “regardless,” but less formal.

〜であれ…であれ: Broader universality, less focused on irrelevance.

Cultural and Practical Notes

Reflects fairness in formal Japanese. Essential for N1 legal/formal texts.
