〜といわず…といわず

Explanation in Original Language

〜も…も区別なく全部。あらゆる所。いつも、同じようだ。

Meaning and Nuances

This means “whether 〜 or …,” implying everything/all places/all times are uniformly affected, with no distinction. It emphasizes totality.

Usage in Context

Used in expressive speech or writing to stress universality, often dramatically.

Examples

子供といわず大人といわず、皆が楽しんでいた。

Children and adults alike—everyone was enjoying it.

Event description.

家の中といわず外といわず、雪で覆われた。

Inside and outside the house—everything was covered in snow.

Weather exaggeration.

朝といわず夜といわず、彼は働き続けた。

Morning or night—he kept working nonstop.

Work ethic praise.

Comparison to Similar Structures

〜といい…といい: Limited to two examples, not totality.

〜も…も: Less emphatic about universality.

Cultural and Practical Notes

Reflects dramatic flair in Japanese. Useful for N1 expressive writing.
