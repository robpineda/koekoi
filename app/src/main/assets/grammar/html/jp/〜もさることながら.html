〜もさることながら

Explanation in Original Language

〜もそうだが、それに追加して、もっと強調したいことがある。

Meaning and Nuances

This acknowledges 〜 as notable but shifts focus to something else deemed more significant. It balances recognition with emphasis, often politely.

Usage in Context

Used in formal or polite speech/writing to introduce a key point after mentioning something obvious.

Examples

味もさることながら、盛り付けが美しい。

The taste is great, but the presentation is especially beautiful.

Food review.

彼の実力もさることながら、人柄が素晴らしい。

His skill is impressive, but his character is even more remarkable.

Personal praise.

価格もさることながら、品質が信頼できる。

The price is good, but the quality is particularly reliable.

Product evaluation.

Comparison to Similar Structures

〜はもちろん: Simpler “of course,” without shifting emphasis.

〜だけでなく: Broader “not only,” less focused on priority.

Cultural and Practical Notes

Reflects polite Japanese discourse of balancing praise. Useful for N1 polite expression.
