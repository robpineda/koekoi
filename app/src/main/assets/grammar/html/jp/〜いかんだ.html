〜いかんだ

Explanation in Original Language

〜がどうであるかによって事態が変わる・事態が決まる。

Meaning and Nuances

This means “depending on 〜,” where the outcome hinges on how 〜 turns out. It’s formal and implies contingency.

Usage in Context

Used in formal discussions or writing to analyze variables affecting a situation.

Examples

君の努力いかんだ、この計画は成功する。

This plan’s success depends on your effort.

Motivation.

天候いかんで、試合が中止になるかもしれない。

The game might be canceled depending on the weather.

Event planning.

資金いかんで、プロジェクトの規模が決まる。

The project’s scale will be determined by the funding.

Business analysis.

Comparison to Similar Structures

〜次第だ: Similar “depending on,” but more common and less formal.

〜によって: Broader “by,” less focused on outcome.

Cultural and Practical Notes

Formal tone suits analytical Japanese discourse. Key for N1 formal reading.
