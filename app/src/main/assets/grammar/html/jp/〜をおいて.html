〜をおいて

Explanation in Original Language

〜のほかに、同じぐらい高く評価できるものはいない・ない。

Meaning and Nuances

This asserts that aside from 〜, there is nothing/no one else equally worthy of high regard or consideration. It emphasizes uniqueness or supremacy in a formal tone.

Usage in Context

Used in formal praise or to single out something exceptional, often in writing or speeches.

Examples

この町をおいて、他にこんな美しい場所はない。

Aside from this town, there’s no place as beautiful.

Tourism or local pride.

彼をおいて、この仕事を任せられる人はいない。

Other than him, there’s no one we can entrust with this job.

Professional endorsement.

母をおいて、私をこんなに愛してくれる人はいない。

Apart from my mother, no one loves me this much.

Personal sentiment.

Comparison to Similar Structures

〜以外に: Simpler “other than,” without the high praise nuance.

〜に勝る: Focuses on superiority, not exclusivity.

Cultural and Practical Notes

Ties to Japan’s value of recognizing unique excellence. Key for N1 formal reading.
