〜に至るまで

Explanation in Original Language

〜という意外なことにまで、あることの範囲が及ぶ。

Meaning and Nuances

This indicates that something extends to an unexpected or extreme point (〜), emphasizing the breadth or inclusiveness of a range. It often carries a tone of surprise or exaggeration about how far something reaches.

Usage in Context

Common in written or formal speech to highlight the scope of influence, impact, or inclusion, often in detailed descriptions.

Examples

小学生に至るまで、スマートフォンを使うようになった。

Even elementary school students have started using smartphones.

Social commentary on technology.

王様から庶民に至るまで、彼の死を悼んだ。

From the king to the common people, everyone mourned his death.

Historical or dramatic narrative.

大都市から田舎の小さな村に至るまで、祭りが開催された。

Festivals were held from big cities to small rural villages.

Nationwide event description.

Comparison to Similar Structures

〜まで: Simpler “up to,” without the unexpected or extreme nuance.

〜にわたって: Focuses on duration or span, not the surprising endpoint.

Cultural and Practical Notes

Reflects a Japanese tendency to emphasize inclusivity or extremes in formal rhetoric. Key for N1 reading passages with detailed scope.
