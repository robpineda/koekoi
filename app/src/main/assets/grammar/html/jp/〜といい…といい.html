〜といい…といい

Explanation in Original Language

〜を見ても…を見ても同じような状態だ。

Meaning and Nuances

This indicates that both 〜 and … exhibit the same quality or condition, often with admiration or criticism. It highlights consistency across examples.

Usage in Context

Used in speech or writing to evaluate multiple aspects, often positively or negatively.

Examples

顔といい性格といい、彼は完璧だ。

His face and personality—both are perfect.

Praise.

態度といい言葉遣いといい、失礼極まりない。

His attitude and language—both are extremely rude.

Criticism.

デザインといい機能といい、この製品は優れている。

Design and functionality—this product excels in both.

Product review.

Comparison to Similar Structures

〜も…も: Broader “both,” less focused on evaluation.

〜といわず…といわず: Covers all aspects, not just two.

Cultural and Practical Notes

Reflects a tendency to assess holistically. Useful for N1 nuanced expression.
