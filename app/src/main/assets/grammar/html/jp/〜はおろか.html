〜はおろか

Explanation in Original Language

〜はもちろん、程度が違うほかのことにも同じことが言える。

Meaning and Nuances

This means “let alone 〜” or “not to mention 〜,” indicating that something applies not just to an extreme case (〜) but also to lesser ones. It often has a negative or ironic tone.

Usage in Context

Common in speech and writing to emphasize inability or contrast, often critically.

Examples

英語はおろか、日本語すらまともに話せない。

He can’t even speak Japanese properly, let alone English.

Criticism of language skills.

走るはおろか、歩くのもやっとだ。

I can barely walk, let alone run.

Physical limitation.

大学はおろか、高校にも行っていない。

He didn’t even go to high school, much less university.

Educational critique.

Comparison to Similar Structures

〜どころか: Similar “far from,” but more casual and versatile.

〜はもちろん: Positive “of course,” lacking the negative nuance.

Cultural and Practical Notes

Reflects a rhetorical style of exaggeration for effect. Useful for N1 conversational contrast.
